Recipe #1. Hamburgers

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: To eat
Ingredients Quantity
  • Meat
  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Yellow cheese
  • Oil
  • Ham
  • 250 grams of meat
  • Mayonnaise and ketchup to taste
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of yellow cheese
  • 1 slice of ham
  • A little oil

Process #1:

Steps:

  1. Put the meat in a pan with oil.
  2. When the meat is almost all browned, put the slice of ham and yellow cheese on top of it.
  3. Once the meat is ready, spread the bread with the mayonnaise and ketchup.
  4. You now have a simple burger to enjoy

Recipe #2. Synchronized

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: To eat
Ingredients Quantity
  • Flour tortilla
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 slice of ham
  • 1 flour tortilla

Process #2:

Steps:

  1. Heat the flour tortilla
  2. Once the tortilla is heated, add the slice of ham and cheese.
  3. Finally, we fold the tortilla in half.
  4. You now have a synchronized to enjoy

Recipe #3. Omelette

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: To eat
Ingredients Quantity
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • A little oil

Process #3:

Steps:

  1. Beat the eggs in a deep plate
  2. Add salt to the batter
  3. Add the batter to a pan with hot oil.
  4. When it starts to sew, add the slice of cheese
  5. When the cheese melts, fold the egg in half.
  6. You now have an omelette to enjoy

Recipe #4. Red spaghetti

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: To eat
Ingredients Quantity
  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato puree
  • Sausage
  • White cheese
  • Water
  • 1 package of spaghetti
  • 4 boxes of 200 ml of tomato puree
  • 6 sausages
  • 2 slices of white cheese
  • 1 liter of water

Process #4:

Steps:

  1. Add the liter of water to a pot to boil
  2. While the water boils, we can cut the sausages and cheese slices into squares
  3. Once the water is boiling, we add the spaghett so that it cooks
  4. Once the spaghetti is cooked, we remove the water
  5. Now, we add all the tomato puree and the sausage and cheese squares
  6. We let it heat up for 5 minutes and that's it, you have a red spaghetti to enjoy.
